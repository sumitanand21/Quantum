Note
Your Contract goes through various stages of review within the organization once you have submitted the request. These stages are 1.Contract-Review request. 2.Contract-Agreement approval.
The “Status” column indicates the system generated stage; To understand the “system generated stage” of your Contract Review Request better, refer to the below explanations of the “Status” column: Draft – Your Contract Review Request has been received by the Contract Review Team. They will check the request and start working on it. If the Contract Review Team finds any issue in the Contract Review Request which you have submitted, they will send the Request back to you. You will receive an email notification which will inform you the reason, for returning it to you. The status in Trace CRM will continue to be in Draft. You are expected to correct the information and submit it again.
